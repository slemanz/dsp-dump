# Complex Numbers

Equation responsible for the existance of complex numbers.

$$ x^2 + 1 = 0 $$

$$ x^2 = -1 $$

$$ x = \sqrt{-1} $$

$$ j = \sqrt{-1} $$

$$ j^2 = -1 $$

Complex Number is the sum of real and imaginary number.

$$z = x +yj$$
